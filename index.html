About Me

Hi my name is Kenji Wagner and welcome to my personal website. A little bit about me, I'm a young, aspiring data scientist and recent graduate from the MS in Data Science program at Boston University, with a BS in Data Science from the University of Michigan, where I also pursued a minor in Asian Languages and Cultures. I’m actively seeking roles in data science, data analytics, data engineering, backend development, or machine learning engineering, where I can apply my experience in building robust ML pipelines, deploying end-to-end data systems, and uncovering insights from complex datasets for using machine learning, natural language processing, and time series modeling to uncover insights and solve real-world problems. Throughout my academic and professional experiences, led projects ranging from consumer sentiment forecasting to building poker bots with deep reinforcement learning to doing data analysis for National Public Radio, supplemented by BU Spark. I enjoy working across the full data pipeline—from cleaning and exploring messy datasets to building and evaluating predictive models.
